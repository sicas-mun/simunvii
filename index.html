About SIMUN VII

SIMUN VII is the official Model United Nations conference of SICAS Liberty Campus, Lahore. Guided by the school’s timeless motto,

“Knowledge Reigns Supreme”

the conference embodies a spirit of inquiry, dialogue, and intellectual growth. Over the years, SIMUN has established itself as a premier forum for young leaders to engage in diplomacy, debate global issues, and craft innovative solutions.

This year’s edition, held from 24–26 October 2025, promises an immersive experience of collaboration, diplomacy, and excellence, leaving delegates with lifelong memories and friendships.
